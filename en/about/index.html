About

Hi, I'm walker.oh — a 40-something who commutes by bike from Ilsan to Sangam-dong in Seoul. I'm no expert; I just love riding, running, wandering off, and tinkering with gear, so I write it all down.

Mornings I fold and unfold a Brompton along the Han River. Weekends I lace up running shoes or shoulder a backpack for the mountains. Sometimes I toss the bike in the trunk of a 4 Gran Coupé and head somewhere far. I buy a bit too much gear… and then regret it ^^;

What you'll find here

  • Brompton & minivelo — real-world reviews, lightweight/gear-ratio/dress-up tuning, bike commuting
  • Cycling near Seoul — Han River, Bukhan River, Anyangcheon routes, night rides, long distance
  • Running — Sangam and Ilsan Lake Park routes, steady running in my 40s
  • Hiking & backpacking — ridge traverses, minimal gear, beginner tips
  • Camping & travel — wild camping, trips like Hokkaido (Sapporo, Otaru)
  • Gear reviews — Garmin watches, bone-conduction earphones, honest month-long use

I'd rather give you an honest review of something I bought and used for a long time than an ad. If it helps someone facing the same choice, that's enough.

I write in Korean but translate into English and Japanese too. If you share the same hobbies, you're welcome here from anywhere.
