Privacy Policy

This policy explains how ohwalker.com ("the site") handles personal data. Effective: 2026-06-21

1. Information we collect

The site has no sign-up or comments, so we don't directly collect names or contact details. When you visit, the following may be collected automatically: cookies, browser/device info, access logs, and page-view records. If you email us, your email address is used only to reply. When you post a comment, the name and text you enter are stored publicly on the site, and a hash of your IP (the raw IP is not stored) is recorded to prevent spam.

2. Cookies & advertising

This site uses cookies.

  • Google Analytics: cookies used to analyze visit statistics.
  • Google AdSense: third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads to you.

View counter: per-post view counts are recorded via Cloudflare as anonymous aggregate numbers only, without cookies, and do not identify individuals.

You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party cookies at aboutads.info. You may also block or delete cookies in your browser settings.

3. Third-party services

The site uses Google Analytics, Google AdSense, GitHub Pages (hosting), and Cloudflare (DNS, view counts). Each follows its own privacy policy.

4. Data retention & sharing

We do not sell or share collected statistics with third parties beyond analytics purposes.

5. Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their information.

6. Contact

Privacy inquiries: bumseok.view@gmail.com

7. Changes

This policy may change; updates will be posted on this page.
